Biography

Patrick S. Phelan, B.A. (Psychology, University of Arkansas '14)

I am a medical student at Washington University School of Medicine in St. Louis. I recently completed my core clinical (third) year and am currently a graduate student studying clinical epidemiology toward a Master of Population Health Sciences (M.P.H.S.) degree during this 2018-2019 academic year, with dual-degree (M.D., M.P.H.S.) graduation anticipated for May 2020.

My clinical interests include general and preventive medicine, dermatology, urology, infectious disease, and nutrition and metabolism. My research interests include clinical dermatology, methodology in evidence-based medicine and biostatistics, and clinical reasoning. Other interests include medical ethics and medical education.
