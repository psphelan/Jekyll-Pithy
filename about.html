Biography

Patrick S. Phelan, B.A. (Psychology, University of Arkansas '14)

I am a third-year medical student at Washington University School of Medicine in St. Louis. I will be pursuing additional education and training through graduate study in clinical epidemiology toward a Master of Population Health Sciences (M.P.H.S.) degree in the coming academic year, with dual-degree (M.D., M.P.H.S.) graduation anticipated for May 2020.

My clinical interests include general and preventive medicine, dermatology, infectious disease, and nutrition and metabolism. My research interests include cutaneous oncology, methodology in evidence-based medicine and biostatistics, and clinical reasoning. Other interests include medical ethics and medical education.
